# Brag Plan: Fitzo

## What is this app?
Fitzo is a gym companion for Indian lifters — workout logging, dal-to-biryani nutrition tracking, gym check-ins with streaks and leaderboards, and an AI coach that reads *all* of it before it says a word.

## The angle
Every fitness app has an "AI coach." Fitzo's actually looks at your data. The video's premise: **receipts.** Fitzo literally prints your training like a thermal receipt ("the weight of 17 auto-rickshaws"), maps your muscles into growth zones, and calls you out when you skip legs. Proof over promises — the whole video is the app showing what it knows about you.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
Pure black. A cream thermal receipt prints downward into frame — mono type ticking out: `FITZO® · TOTAL WEIGHT MOVED · 12,480 KG · "The weight of 17 auto-rickshaws"`. Printer-tick SFX. Nobody else's fitness app prints receipts with auto-rickshaws on them; it earns the next 18 seconds instantly.

## Key moments (the middle)
- **The coach knows.** A chat bubble types out a reply that could only come from real data: "Bench up 7.5kg this month. But you've skipped legs 12 days — today's the day. 🦵" with small context chips (14-day volume · sleep · streak 12🔥) beneath.
- **The heatmap turning green.** Front/back anatomy silhouettes; muscles flip amber → green one by one as a "sets this week" counter ticks up; "Growth zone" label lands on the beat.
- **Dal to biryani + the gym floor.** Food log card: "2 roti · dal · 100g paneer" types in, macros count up instantly. Beside it the gym leaderboard slides in — #1 gets a 👊 kudos pop.

## Outro / punchline
The geometric F logo mark stamps in like a printer head, wordmark "FITZO" beside it. Line: **"The coach that actually knows you."** Under it, quiet: `Early access → fitzoapp.in`.

## User flow worth showing
Log a workout (sets tick in) → the coach reacts with a data-specific insight → the heatmap goes green and the receipt prints for sharing. Entry → action → result: the centerpiece scenes ARE this loop; the receipt is both hook and payoff.

## Tone
- Preset: polished
- Creative direction: "quiet premium product film in Onyx & Snow — PUSH-style confidence with Indian personality"
- Interpretation: fewer scenes, longer settled holds, monochrome restraint; energy comes from precise motion (printing, typing, counting, muscle flips) synced to beats — never from flashing text or color noise.

## Format: landscape — 1920x1080
## Duration: 21 seconds

## Visual identity (from the project)
- Background: #000000 (pure black; surfaces #0A0A0A)
- Accent: white #FFFFFF hierarchy (70/55/45% alphas); functional colors only where the app uses them — growth green #34D159, under-target amber #FDC90D, streak flame 🔥
- Receipt palette: cream paper #F1EEE6, ink #141414, hand-drawn pen blue #2B5CE6
- Text: #FFFFFF primary, rgba(255,255,255,0.7) secondary
- Display font: Lexend (700/500, tight tracking) — the app's font
- Body font: Lexend 400; receipt uses monospace (VT323-style thermal look)
- Strongest visual element: the thermal receipt share card + the anatomy heatmap with growth zones (both are real app components)

## Share copy (draft)
Your gym app prints receipts now. Fitzo — the AI coach that actually reads your training (and knows you skipped legs). Early access → fitzoapp.in

## Audio direction
- Role: warm confident bed with motion-matched accents
- Music: `happy-beats-business-moves-vol-9-by-ende-dot-app.mp3` (114.84 BPM, 1:54)
- Music treatment: start at 0s low, settle under scenes, small lift into the heatmap scene, resolve + fade over the outro logo stamp
- Music cue guidance: preset cues read from `assets/music/cues/vol-9`. Strong cues to target: **4.23s** (receipt → wordmark reveal), **10.54s** (heatmap flip begins), **12.65s** (Growth-zone label lands). Beat grid ≈ every 0.52s — use every OTHER beat (~1.05s) for sequential muscle flips/counter ticks; sequential TEXT holds ≥0.8s settled.
- Audio-reactive treatment: none — polished restraint; motion syncs to beats but nothing pulses to the waveform
- SFX posture: sparse, motion-matched — printer ticks (hook), soft keyboard ticks (coach typing), gentle UI pops (muscle flips, kudos), one clean logo stamp
- Audio-coupled moments: receipt printing, coach reply typing, per-muscle flips on the half-beat grid, macro counter tick-up, kudos pop, final stamp
- Restraint rule: no risers, no whoosh spam, no sound on transitions unless a scene element moves; text never exits before its settled hold

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — The receipt prints — 4s
Black frame. Cream receipt card scrolls down from top-center as if printing (masked reveal, slight paper tilt −0.6°): `FITZO®` header, black bar `TOTAL WEIGHT MOVED`, barbell mark, `12,480 KG` in thermal mono, caption `The weight of 17 auto-rickshaws`, dashed rule, `Total ................ 12,480 kg` with a hand-drawn blue ink circle drawing itself around the total.
Sequential/interaction: yes — receipt content reveals top-to-bottom as it "prints"; ink circle draws on last.
Audio intent: mechanical intimacy — you hear the printer before you know what app this is.
Audio-coupled idea: printer ticks per printed block; soft pen scribble as the circle draws.
Music: enters at 0s, low and warm.
Transition mood: clean (receipt slides up and away) → Scene 2

### Scene 2 — Wordmark reveal — 3s
On the 4.23s strong cue: geometric F icon (black rounded square, white angular F on white-bordered tile so it reads on black) scales in with a stamp; "FITZO" in Lexend 700 tight-tracked lands beside it. Below, settled hold: **"The coach that actually knows you."** (holds ≥1.8s).
Sequential/interaction: none — one confident composite reveal.
Audio intent: arrival; the brand hits on the beat.
Audio-coupled idea: single soft stamp/impact on the cue.
Transition mood: soft crossfade → Scene 3

### Scene 3 — The coach knows — 5s
Dark phone frame (Onyx UI, glass cards). User bubble slides in: "kya karu aaj?" Coach reply **types out**: "Bench up 7.5 kg this month. But you've skipped legs 12 days — today's the day 🦵". Three context chips fade in beneath the reply, one per half-beat: `14-day volume` · `sleep 7.2h` · `streak 12 🔥`. Left of the phone, headline settles: **"An AI coach that reads your training."**
Sequential/interaction: yes — simulated chat: bubble in, reply typed character-by-character, chips arrive one by one.
Audio intent: intelligence at work; quiet keys, no drama.
Audio-coupled idea: keyboard ticks during typing; soft pop per chip.
Transition mood: slide → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — Growth zones — 5s
The app's real anatomy heatmap: front + back silhouettes (organic muscle paths) on black. On the 10.54s cue, muscles flip amber → green one by one on the half-beat grid (chest, delts, lats, quads, hams) while a counter ticks `sets this week: 2 → 8`. On the 12.65s cue the label **"Growth zone"** lands in green with a settled hold. Headline: **"See where you're growing."**
Sequential/interaction: yes — per-muscle color flips in sequence; counter ticks up.
Audio intent: satisfaction; each flip is a tiny win.
Audio-coupled idea: soft UI pop per muscle flip on the half-beat; counter tick.
Transition mood: slide → Scene 5

### Scene 5 — Dal to biryani + outro — 4s
Split moment, then close. Food card types "2 roti · dal · 100g paneer" → macros count up (P 42g · C 68g · F 18g); gym leaderboard card slides in beside it, row #1 receives a 👊 kudos pop (+1). Cards recede; the F mark **stamps** center-frame with wordmark, line: **"Early access → fitzoapp.in"** (settled ≥1.5s). Music resolves and fades.
Sequential/interaction: yes — typed food entry, counting macros, kudos tap simulated, final stamp.
Audio intent: warmth then resolution; end clean, no tail noise.
Audio-coupled idea: type ticks, counter, one kudos pop, final stamp hit; music fade-out over last 1s.
Transition mood: soft → end

**Music mood for this video:** upbeat-warm, confident (vol-9 @ ~115 BPM)
**Audio summary:** printer ticks open in near-silence, the bed settles in low, brand lands on a strong cue, quiet keys and half-beat UI pops carry the middle, and a single stamp with a music resolve closes it.
