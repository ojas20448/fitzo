Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Fitzo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Fitzo.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password.
  • Health Data: Height, weight, gender, age, workout logs, nutrition logs. (Stored securely and never sold).
  • Usage Data: Interaction with gym features, completion of lessons.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide functionality like:

  • Calculating your TDEE and nutrition targets.
  • Tracking your workout progress over time.
  • Enabling trainer-client interactions.

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data. You can delete your account from within the app settings or by contacting us at support@fitzo.app.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for database hosting and Render for API hosting. Both providers adhere to strict security standards.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fitzo.app.
